Homelessness in NYC

Homelessness Statistics

In New York City, One in every 120 New Yorkers are homeless. 3,400 homeless people live in the subways.

Drug use amoung homeless people

In NYC, Drug use is a very big issue. Lots of homeless people are either homeless because of drugs or got addicted after becoming homeless.Theres also lots of violence and death due to drugs.
